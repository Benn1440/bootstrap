OUR SERVICES

Assembly Plant

We manage the CKD and SKD assembly of all asset types at own sites as well as outsourced sites within Nigeria. We offer full assembly services for trucks, trailers and buses.

Sales & Dealership

Our strong partnership and collaboration with OEM Partners such as SCANIA, Marcopolo, Continental Tyres and Kassbohrer ensure that our clients get the best products off the shelf at reasonable prices

Maintenance, Repairs & Overhaul (MRO)

We provide full range of after sales services including truck maintenance, repairs and engine overhaul. We combine both local and foreign expertise to bring an edge to our MRO service delivery.

Check Out Our Offers

Procurement Agency

We provide and manage all inventories with respect to spare parts supplies to all points of presence (irrespective of location). This also covers strategic sourcing, supply levels, as well as sales of retail parts. We partner with clients to provide the necessary spare parts to aid the optimal usage of their assets. We order strategically, source and deliver the procured items straight to clients’ doorsteps.

Delivery Management

We have a dedicated team of delivery professionals that manage various aspects of tyres and are also responsible for providing pre-purchase advisory, sales, usage analysis and tyre performance, throughout the entire product life cycle. Our delivery management service is not restricted to just the vehicles we manage as we also offer retreading services for all types of operations.

Driving &Technical

We have a technical academy centre of excellence that provides a one-stop shop solution through skills and capability development to support the automotive value chain. We develop training solutions, specifically for transport and logistics industry. Our focus areas include: drivers and mechatronic trainings, driver evaluation and verification, company drivers’ and vehicle safety assessment for corporate bodies, drivers recruitment and transport advisory.

Why Choose Us

We pride ourselves in being the one-stop shop for the design, sales and procurement of new trucks,
trailers, spare parts for fleets, general tyre management, including importation and assembly of trucks,
trailers and buses, as well as maintenance, repair & overhaul of trucks/trailers.
